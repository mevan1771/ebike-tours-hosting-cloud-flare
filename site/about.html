Our story

We started Ceylon E‑Bike Tours to share Sri Lanka’s warmth, history and biodiversity in a low‑impact way. Our team combines guiding, safety and storytelling to create easy‑going adventures for curious travelers.

From the ancient cities of the Cultural Triangle to misty tea country and the southern coastline, e‑bikes let you see more while treading lightly.

Values

Safety first

Training, briefings and quality kit on every ride.

Local impact

Community guides and small businesses benefit directly.

Respect nature

Low‑emission transport and leave‑no‑trace practices.
