Adam’s Peak Cycle & Sacred Climb

Quiet tea roads around Maskeliya & Norton Bridge — sacred summit of Sri Pada

8–10 hrs Moderate–Challenging Ride 15–25 km + sacred climb Start: Nallathanniya / Maskeliya
Book now See route

Why you'll love it

Experience the majesty of Sri Lanka's Central Highlands in this ultimate adventure, combining a scenic cycle and a sacred climb. Pedal through the emerald tea plantations that blanket the hillsides, circling the serene Maskeliya Reservoir with breathtaking views. Then, ditch the bike and join a timeless pilgrimage, ascending the 5,000+ steps of Sri Pada (Adam's Peak) in the pre-dawn darkness. Your reward is witnessing the world-famous sunrise and the mountain's perfectly triangular shadow—a sight of unparalleled natural and spiritual beauty. This is a balanced, challenging, and profoundly rewarding experience designed for all levels of fit adventurers.

Step 1

Welcome at Glengariff Villa & Gear Preparation

Your journey begins at the tranquil Glengariff Villa. Get a warm welcome, receive your high-quality mountain bike, and undergo a comprehensive bike fit. A final briefing covers the day's route, safety protocols, and the logistics for the later night trek.
Step 2

Cycling the Upper Tea Country near Norton Bridge

Set off on your cycle through the heart of the Ceylon Tea Country. This initial leg takes you through the lush, low-lying roads of the Norton Bridge area. Enjoy the crisp mountain air and the captivating sight of tea pluckers at work in the endless emerald landscapes.
Step 3

The Panoramic Ride to Nallathanniya (Dalhousie)

The cycling climax! The route transitions to breathtaking panoramic views as you circle the azure waters of the Maskeliya Reservoir. This stunning section delivers you directly to Nallathanniya, the bustling base village and the official trailhead for Adam's Peak.
Step 4

Pre-Climb Rest, Refreshment & Pilgrimage Initiation

Secure your bikes and check into local accommodation near the base. Enjoy a hearty early dinner and essential rest. Our guides will brief you on the sacred significance of Sri Pada and confirm the midnight/pre-dawn start time for the trek.
Step 5

The Summit Challenge: Night Ascent to Sunrise

Begin the 5,000+ step ascent. Experience the incredible camaraderie of the pilgrimage path, stopping at traditional tea houses for warmth. Your reward is arriving at the summit before dawn to witness the majestic sunrise and the profound moment when the peak casts its famous triangular shadow.
Step 6

Daylight Descent, Well-Earned Rest & Wrap-Up

The descent allows you to appreciate the jungle and mountain scenery missed in the dark. Upon returning to Nallathanniya, enjoy refreshments and a final Sri Lankan breakfast before we arrange your transfer, concluding a truly monumental tour.
