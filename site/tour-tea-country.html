Adam’s Peak Cycle & Sacred Climb

Quiet tea roads around Maskeliya & Norton Bridge — sacred summit of Sri Pada

8–10 hrs Moderate–Challenging Ride 15–25 km + sacred climb Start: Nallathanniya / Maskeliya
Book now See route

Why you'll love it

Ride emerald tea lanes skirting Maskeliya Lake, then ascend the sacred steps of Adam’s Peak (Sri Pada) for sky‑lit views. We keep the cycling on quiet estate byways and reserve your legs for the pilgrimage climb. Expect cool mountain air, bell chimes at dawn, steaming tea at roadside stalls and a satisfying downhill roll past plantations and reservoirs.

Step 1

Meet at Nallathanniya · bike fit & safety brief

Fitting your e‑bike and going over the route, weather and sacred‑site etiquette. Warm‑up spin along Maskeliya Lake.
Step 2

Sacred climb to Adam’s Peak (Sri Pada)

Secure bikes at the trailhead and ascend the famous steps with your guide. Bells, shrines and sweeping views reward the effort. Descend carefully and hydrate.
Step 3

Breakfast & tea‑estate roll‑out

Refuel on local roti/tea, then glide through plantations on quiet backroads. Camera‑ready valleys and ridgelines.
Step 4

Reservoir viewpoints & waterfall stop

Roll along the lakeshore near Norton Bridge and pause at a nearby cascade when flow allows. Safety first during wet conditions.
Step 5

Village lunch & temple courtesy stop

Simple rice‑and‑curry or snacks depending on timing. Shoulders covered for temple grounds; your guide provides sarongs if needed.
Step 6

Lakeside return & tea tasting

Spin back along calm roads, stopping for a fresh Ceylon tea tasting before we wrap. Vehicle transfer available on request.
